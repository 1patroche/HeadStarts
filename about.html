About Us

HeadStarts was setup in 2011 to prepare students for all aspects of college life and beyond. Our dedicated team of educators have years of experience in education, life sciences and Post-School training. At HeadStarts, we aim to provide a vibrant and engaging environment where students can learn to prepare for all the rigors and challenges of college life to come.

Our surveys have shown that students who have attended Headstarts pre-college courses have a 45% better chance at graduating their course than other students

Contact us now to find out about courses available in your area. We currently have centers in Wexford, Waterford, Cork and Carlow
