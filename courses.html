Course Details

We have tailor made our courses to suit the most important skills that students will need to master before entering into third-level exducation.

Our average class lasts for 1 hour and ranges from 5 students up to a maximum of 15. We keep our class numbers small to ensure that every student gets ample time and opportunity to engage with our educators.

Most classes typically begin in June but certain training is available all year round and arrangements can be made if required.

If you would like to talk to us about any of our courses, please visit our Contact page for details
