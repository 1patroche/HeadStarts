Pre-College Preparation

Headstarts is a unique pre-college program for students about to attend college or university

We provide essential training and information on all aspects of both academic, home and social life while attending a third-level course

Choose from in-depth course in ICT (Information and Communications Technology), Study & Research methodology, Home Economics, Time & task management, Basic Accountancy, First-aid and many more
